# 00-product-overview.md

## Purpose
What ROOTS is, what it sells, who it serves, and what v1 must achieve.

## 1. Product Summary
ROOTS is a premium UK pharmacy and wellness platform combining one flagship consultation-led medicine journey (Mounjaro) with a small supporting range of wellness supplements. Replaces an existing Shopify storefront — must visually match it while implementing custom clinical, commerce, and fulfillment workflows.

## 2. v1 Product Scope
### Flagship: Mounjaro Weight Management Programme
One product, multiple dose variants: 2.5mg, 5mg, 7.5mg, 10mg, 12.5mg, 15mg

### Supplements
Magnesium Glycinate, Electrolytes, Probiotic (women's health), Sleep support, optional Collagen/Vitamin D

### Rules
No subscriptions. All one-off purchases. No recurring billing.

## 3. Regulatory Prerequisites
- GPhC registration (confirmed — partner is registered)
- Stripe pharmaceutical waiver (apply before processing POM payments)
- CQC registration for prescribing service if applicable

## 4. Out of Scope for v1
Subscriptions, AI, direct Royal Mail API (use Click & Drop API), support role, multiple POM programs, marketing automation, multi-warehouse.

## 5. Success Criteria
Homepage matches Shopify. Full Mounjaro journey works end to end. Supplements work. Prescriber can review. Admin can fulfill. Emails cover all states. Platform scales to thousands of users.
